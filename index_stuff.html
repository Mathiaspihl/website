About This Site and ...Stuff

So, nice that you dropped by! The idea with this is really to learn, share ideas and thoughts and - at best - knowledge. It all started when I was checking out how to set up and website (with CMS-type functions) using Netlify and GitHub. One thing led to another and it became an interesting "project". At one point you have to just try it out for your self...

I like the idea of K.I.S.S - Keep It Stupid Simple. This, I think, doesn't implicate the lack of functionality. Like Einstein said, "Everything should be made as simple as possible, but no simpler". I love that quote! So with that in mind, I wanted to go back to the basics with this site. Creating everything myself from scratch in a text editor, making it lean and mean. Going back to the roots, like how I started out back in the early '90s. That said; I did a littlebit of research and found that with VSC text editor, Netlify and GitHub I would cover the hosting and also the CMS-type of requirements. Of course; it's work in progress and I'm still tweaking the process etc.

On another level, I felt it's actually needed in times of Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Snapchat, TikTok...and I feel everything is just snowballing. The digital footprint it consumes and the social influence and impact it has on all of us is just crazy!
