3rd and 4th of Nov 2023

Sun Nov 5 2023, 12:03:58 am CET Today was one of those day when it feels like you never really wake up. Just grey, rainy and windy outside - fitting weather matching the mood perfectly.

I was trying to get the NAS safety backup job to run successfully, but for what ever reason it is running endlessly until it completely hangs. The only way to revive it is a proper hard reboot; pulling the plug thus. I have been peeking to a new solution, either building my own or buying a new one or, the third option is a hybrid of both of those options (buying an old rack server and modify it). Not sure just yet...

Another very exciting thing today was the latest Polestar car update, the P2.12 was successfully installed over OTA (Over-the-Air). It's the first update after the regular service appointment that I had some time back, I was hoping it would work after that (or if I had to go back to the garage to get it). It didn't just work smoothly, I see that I received it VERY early. So that's great!

Ohhhh, I almost forgot, we are getting fiber next week. Today we received the message that we have to clear out the street, the driveway and inform them (meaning the internet, phone and TV provider company) where they can dig for the fiber cables. The electricity- and phone cables, gas, water and sewer tubes are placed is not the easiest to know were they are located. We check all the plans and drawings, but it is not mentioned anywhere. One would think that THEY would have that information, especially since they are the ones doing the digging...

With that uplifting and inspiring posts, I will retire to the comfort of my bed to enjoy a good book.

29th of Oct until 2nd of Nov 2023

Thu Nov 2 2023, 3:12:33 pm CET Yesterday we arrived back home again after a few wonderful days in Prague. It is an absolutely beautiful city and I enjoyed every second being together with the best travel companions one could wish for. I took loads of pictures and recorded a lot of videos of the beautiful Prague and everything we did there. I have not yet had the time to look through it in detail as we arrived yesterday evening and I was back to work this morning.

Work... at first, it was difficult to get back into it but coffee made it certainly easier; ensuring a little energy boost and inner warmth. Oh, that was the poetry of the day.

The weather is absolutely terrible, we are having the worst storm here at the moment. It is especially stressful for someone having solar panels on the roof. Every time there is a gust, I am just waiting to see a solar panel passing by my window...

OK, now I have to return to my office, a call is about to start...
