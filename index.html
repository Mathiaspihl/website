7th until 8th of April 2024

Mon Apr 8 2024, 7:13:43 pm CET Today was an exceptionally long day. Yes, it started already at 03:30 when the alarm clock went off. At 04:00 we - the youths as well - were all sitting in the car heading for the airport in Eindhoven. I hardly slept at all during the already short night. And I can surely relate to how it must feel for the Zombies...

At least the youths were set off in perfect time, the were safely boarding in time all was good. Until they arrived at the hotel in Marrakesh... The hotel was no more. They were informed that it's been closed for months and there they where. Alone on the street in Marrakesh. Luckely they have booked it via a legit booking agency (Booking.com) which means that they have some sort of service included. Trying to reach the hotel was hopeless, the best bet was with Booking.com, which I suggested. Since it is extremely expensive (and not easily done on the streets of Marrakesh) using their mobile phones, the coordination and communication went to us. Which we happily did. To shorten a VERY long story, we manged to sort everything out thanks to the diplomatic skills of H. and a helpful support guy at Booking.com. So for now our little ones have a safe place to stay until Wednesday. Then we have to hunt for a hotel stay until they leave on monday... The charm of being a parent!
